## Version

v1.0

## Founder

Manas Dey

## Product Name

FitMantra

## Product Type

Mobile-first wellness application

## Platform

iOS + Android

## Primary Market

India

## Target Audience

Men aged 25–45 (initial focus), middle-class Indian users, urban + tier 2 cities

---

# 1. Vision

FitMantra exists to help Indian middle-class users improve their daily health through simple, realistic, low-effort routines designed for busy lifestyles.

The app is not designed for bodybuilders, athletes, or hardcore fitness users.

Instead, FitMantra focuses on:

* energy
* sleep
* stress
* consistency
* daily movement
* lifestyle improvement

The goal is to become:

> “The daily wellness companion for India.”

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Indian middle-class men commonly face:

* chronic fatigue
* poor sleep
* belly fat
* lack of exercise
* stress and burnout
* inconsistent routines
* low motivation
* poor preventive healthcare habits

Existing fitness apps are:

* intimidating
* gym-focused
* overwhelming
* expensive
* unrealistic for working professionals

Users do not want six-pack abs.

Users want:

* more energy
* better sleep
* reduced stress
* healthier habits
* simple routines that fit real life

---

# 3. Product Goals

## Primary Goals

1. Improve user consistency in healthy habits
2. Increase daily engagement through lightweight interactions
3. Create emotionally relatable wellness experience
4. Build strong retention using streaks and progress systems

## Business Goals

1. Reach 10,000 active users in first 6 months
2. Achieve strong Day-30 retention
3. Build organic acquisition through content marketing
4. Prepare infrastructure for subscription monetization
5. Build wellness-focused brand identity
